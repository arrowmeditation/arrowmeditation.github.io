About Us

AIM is a corporation created to fill the need for calm, clarity and well-being among the people working and living in the NYC area. We strive to provide inexpensive or free access to the scientifically proven benefits of meditation through public and private channels. We seek to perpetuate the collective good that comes from regularly meditating, by providing programs designed to teach skills that can last a lifetime - and create a population that can live harmoniously, effectively and relatively free from the effects of stress and conflict. We seek to further the research done to measure the effects of meditation on general well being, health & wellness, productivity, focus and effectiveness. Our goal is to bring wellness in all its forms to to those people that live and work to make NYC great.

Bill Pagano

Bill Pagano

Bill Pagano is a certified instructor in Taoist Meditation, Tai Chi Chuan, Qigong and Ba Gua Zhang. He has been a student and practitioner of the internal martial arts since 1990, and Sifu, or teacher, since 2000. Bill has taught classes, workshops and seminars in London, Frankfurt, and New York City, and at summer retreats with The Healing Tao in upstate NY. Bill has been awarded gold medals in a number of regional tournaments, and has appeared in numerous articles on Taoist arts in martial arts magazines.

Lilli Markle

Lilli Markle

Lilli Markle is a Yoga Alliance Registered Yoga Teacher, Certified Thai Massage Therapist, and is pursuing a certificate in Movement Analysis at the Laban/Bartenieff Institute for Movement Studies. She has incorporated meditation into her personal practice for nearly 10 years, and in more recent years she has integrated the myriad techniques and benefits of meditation into her wellness work. Lilli is drawn to working with artists, helping to guide them to their intuitive space and discover their inner muse, as well as with anyone working with anxiety, chronic stress, pain and injury, who is on their path toward self-healing.

Monica Snavely

Monica Snavely

Monica is a somatic arts educator and personal trainer specializing in mind-body fitness. She has over 10 years of teaching experience and is certified in chair yoga as well as perinatal fitness. Monica became enthralled with meditation while pursuing a MA degree in Dance & Theatre Education in New Mexico. Once she moved to New York City she began taking an in-depth training program in Ayurvedic holistic health coaching. The effects on her own mental, physical, and emotional wellbeing were undeniable. She continued to develop her meditation skills during the five years she spent teaching a Philosophy of Aesthetics class to adolescents to help them balance the tumultuous ups and downs of their daily lives while at the same time facilitating their self-confidence and creativity.

Raj Vansia

Raj Vansia

Raj is a biomedical engineer by training and meditator of 7 years by passion. His meditation style is that each person should find a meditation that aligns with their own nature. Meditation is a great way to explore our bodies and what we truly want in life, it just takes experimenting with different techniques to find the right one. He also uses meditation to help students reach their peak performance, in business, school, and sports. Raj’s expertise is in pranayama and mindfulness. Raj enjoys teaching meditation because he loves the look of relaxation on his students after a great meditation session. As a biomedical engineer he is very interested in the crossroads between mediation and scientific research. He loves to read research articles uncovering the underlying mechanisms of mediation has on our bodies.
