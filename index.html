Arrow Institute of Meditation (AIM) is a fiscally sponsored organization created to fill the need for calm, clarity and well-being among the people working and living in the NYC area. We strive to provide inexpensive or free access to the scientifically proven benefits of meditation through public and private channels. We seek to perpetuate the collective good that comes from regularly meditating, by providing programs designed to teach skills that can last a lifetime - and create a population that can live harmoniously, effectively and relatively free from the effects of stress and conflict. We seek to further the research done to measure the effects of meditation on general well being, health & wellness, productivity, focus and effectiveness. Our goal is to bring wellness in all its forms to those people that live and work to make NYC great.
